To compile: "make".
To execute: " ./library"

This is a library application made by Travis and Oumar, please follow the instructions.

Pressing 1 will allow you to log in and create your account. Pressing 2 will save your login information in the Account.txt file.

Please enter a maximum of 10 characters as only the first 10 characters of the username and password will be taken into account when creating the account.

Books can only be borrowed once and unfortunately cannot be returned.

I hope you find your book and enjoy reading it!!!!!
